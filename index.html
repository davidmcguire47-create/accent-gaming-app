How this is calculated:

Results show the statistical mean (average). A roll of 1 is always treated as a failure for hits and wounds. Target Cover applies a -1 to Hit penalty. Smoke and Stealth apply a -1 to Hit penalty and reduce the attacker's AP by 1. Hit modifiers are capped at -1 total. Save modifiers (AP) apply directly to the Armor Save, but cannot affect the Invulnerable Save. Anti-X triggers Critical Wounds, bypassing Toughness. Devastating Wounds cause Mortals on a Crit, bypassing saves.
